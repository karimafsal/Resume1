 Voice Pathology Detection System (Main Project)

A significant number of people suffer from voice pathology. This pathology can be easily cured if detected early. Here, a voice pathology detection system within a smart healthcare framework is proposed. The inputs are obtained by microphones and electroglottography devices to capture voice and EGG signals. Spectrograms are obtained from these signals and fed into a trained CNN. The features extracted from the CNN are fused and predicts output. The proposed system is evaluated using a publicly available Saarbrucken voice database.
